Frequently Asked Questions

How many leads will you send every week/month/year?

Currently, the agency plan gets a minimum of 10 leads per weekday, and the freelancer plans each get 5. This works out to 2400+ and 1200+ leads per year, respectively.

Can workshop members tailor emails by expertise and project size?

Currently, we're offering plans categorized by agency, design, and development leads. More specific customization and tailored plans are something that is in the works for future versions of Workshop.

Will I be able to make a full-time living off of Workshop leads?

Probably not - atleast not in the first few months. However, you should be able to fill the gaps in your regular work and make sure you have a full pipeline. Workshop is best suited as a supplement to your regular work... but over months and years it can become the main source for work, if you use it properly ie. you are persistent and keep in contact with everyone you email.

How often do leads arrive and at what time?

Leads arrive every weekday at 8:30am EST.

What type of leads do you send?

We send the best freelance opportunities online. We scour the internet, looking at endless amounts of job boards, classifieds, and social media - to hand-pick the best opportunities. The list is curated by hand every day, and we only send leads that are 1) Freelance opportunities 2) Remote friendly, open to freelancers anywhere 3) Quality projects with budgets above $1,000

Can I have my emails be sent once a week, or every other day?

Changing the frequency of the emails in your inbox is currently not offered, however, we may look at adding this in a future version of Workshop.

Are workshop leads right for my skillset?

It depends, the freelance design plan has abotu 10-15% branding and identity work, 20-25% user experience and strategy, 30-40% user interface and web design, and about 20-25% mobile related work. The freelance development package currently features 15-25% mobile related lead, 20-25% rails and app software leads, 30-40% web development leads, and 15-25% front-end development related work. The agency plan includes all of these leads plus one or two agency specific leads per email (larger projects).

How many people will I be competing with?

The amount of Workshop members fluctuates - but generally, leads outnumber members by 26 to 1 per year.
